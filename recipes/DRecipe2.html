Seasoned Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  1. Chicken breast
  2. Garlic Salt
  3. Onion Powder

  • step 1

    Season both sides of the chicken breast with garlic salt and onion powder

  • step 2

    Preheat oven to 425. Put chicken in for 15 minutes, flip, then another 10 minutes.

  • step 3

    Broil the chicken for 4 minutes afterwards.
