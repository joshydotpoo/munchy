Munchy

Omelette with Cheese

Ingredients

  1. 3 Eggs
  2. 1/2 cup Shreded cheese

  • Step 1:

    Whisk eggs in a bowl.

  • Step 2:

    Add eggs to pan on medium heat and let the egg firm up.

  • Step 3:

    Sprinkle cheese over the egg, gently fold when the cheese has melted and the egg is firm.
